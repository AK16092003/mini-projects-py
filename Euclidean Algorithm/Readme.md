Code to execute the Extended Euclidean Algorithm to find the gcd of 2 given numbers.
Also find a^-1 mod b and b^-1 mod a

Example:

```python
1234 5432
+-------+------------+------+------+
|   MOD |   QUOTIENT |   R0 |   R1 |
+=======+============+======+======+
|  1234 |          0 |    1 |    0 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|  5432 |          0 |    0 |    1 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|  1234 |          4 |    1 |    0 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|   496 |          2 |   -4 |    1 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|   242 |          2 |    9 |   -2 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|    12 |         20 |  -22 |    5 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
|     2 |          6 |  449 | -102 |
+-------+------------+------+------+
>>>
```
